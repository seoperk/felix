How Much Is a DHA License in Dubai?

The total cost of obtaining a Dubai Health Authority (DHA) license in Dubai typically falls between AED 3,000 and AED 8,000 for most doctors, nurses, dentists, pharmacists and allied health professionals — depending on your profession, exams required and whether you need additional services such as document attestation or fast-track support.

  • Exam & application fees: usually AED 1,000 – 2,000+
  • DataFlow / PSV: often AED 1,000 – 2,500+ per file
  • License issuance & renewal: roughly AED 1,000 – 2,000+ per year

Quick Summary: DHA License Cost in Dubai

The cost of a DHA professional license is not a single flat number. Instead, the final amount you pay is made up of several line items: DataFlow/PSV fees, exam fees, application charges, license issuance and renewal costs. Additional expenses, such as attestation of overseas documents, courier charges and medical fitness tests, can also affect your budget.

Because each category of healthcare professional (consultant, specialist, general practitioner, registered nurse, technician, etc.) follows a slightly different pathway, it is more useful to think in ranges and components rather than a single price tag.

Hidden or Overlooked Costs

Document Attestation & Translation

Many international clinicians underestimate the cost of attesting academic and professional documents in their home country and in the UAE. Attestation, notarisation and legal translation can add several hundred dirhams per document, particularly for specialists with multiple degrees and fellowships.

Time, Resits and Opportunity Cost

If you need to resit an exam or submit additional documents, you may pay repeat fees and experience a delay in starting work. For senior physicians and experienced nurses, the opportunity cost of a delayed start date can easily outweigh the direct licensing fees.

How DHA License Fees Are Paid

Most DHA license fees are paid online using the official portals linked to the Sheryan system. You will typically pay in AED by credit card or via other accepted e-government channels. DataFlow and exam fees are often settled separately through their respective platforms.

  • Fees are usually non-refundable once a verification or exam booking has been initiated.
  • Exchange rates may apply if your card is linked to a non-AED currency.
  • Facilities sometimes reimburse part of the licensing cost as part of your employment package.

Frequently Asked Questions About DHA License Costs

Is there one fixed price for a DHA license?

No. The cost depends on your profession, the number of qualifications and employers to be verified, whether exams are required, and how many times you sit those exams. That is why ranges are more realistic than a single figure.

Are DHA fees the same as MOHAP or DOH fees?

Each regulator in the United Arab Emirates, including MOHAP and the Department of Health Abu Dhabi (DOH), has its own fee schedule and procedures. Some services may be similarly priced, but they are not identical.

Will my employer reimburse DHA licensing costs?

Many hospitals, clinics and healthcare groups in Dubai partially or fully reimburse professional licensing costs for successful candidates, particularly for in-demand specialties. The details depend on your employment contract and HR policy.
